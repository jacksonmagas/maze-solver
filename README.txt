*----------- Maze Game Documentation -----------*
*                                               *
* Solving Commands:                             *
* Press at the start of the game to choose your *
* desired solution method.                      *
*                                               *
*	-- b: breadth-first search              *
*	-- d: depth-first search                *
*	-- a: A* search                         *
*	-- arrow keys: manual solve             *
*                                               *
* Other Commands:                               *
*	-- n: generate new maze                 *
*	-- r: reset current maze                *
*                                               *
